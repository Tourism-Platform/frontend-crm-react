How the operator workflow works

From building a tour to receiving payment — every step in one system.

1️⃣

Build your tour

Add days, destinations and components from your supplier library. Pricing calculates automatically based on costs.

2️⃣

Publish to agencies

One click publishes your tour to the TourLink catalogue. Agencies across the network can find and book it immediately.

3️⃣

Manage bookings

Confirm, modify or decline requests. All communication tracked. Documents auto-sent to the agency on confirmation.

4️⃣

Track finances

Every booking logged with revenue, costs and margin. Monthly reports generated automatically.

5️⃣

Deliver the tour

Vouchers sent to agencies and tourists. Your team has task checklists. Suppliers notified automatically.

6️⃣

Grow with data

See which tours sell best, which months peak, which agencies bring most revenue. Make better decisions.

Built in Uzbekistan, for Uzbekistan

Our mission

Make Central Asian tourism world-class from the inside out

Uzbekistan has extraordinary tourism potential — Silk Road heritage, stunning landscapes, legendary hospitality. But the operational infrastructure is decades behind.

We started TourLink because we believe local operators deserve the same quality tools as businesses in Europe or Asia. When operators run better, tourists have better experiences. The whole industry wins.
